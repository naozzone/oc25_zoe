Zale's story

Zale is a character based on a shark. In a fantasic world, Zale lives down the ocean with his famaly, he has a strong shark form, after all he's from the royality. He's indeed a shark prince, he's devoted to his own role until time comes that he will be the one ruling the ocean empire. He lives in a magical world where his best friend is living t the surface, whose name is Cainix, he, himself is a dark elves. The whole communities on the earth and the ocean hates humans as a long time ago a war exploded between both of the worlds, since then the humans and the magical communities cut all connections and they never spoke again to avoid another terrible war.
